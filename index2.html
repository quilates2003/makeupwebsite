BASE PRODUCTS


It's always good to practice good makeup base from this products. From this, you can create a flawless base makeup. Starting from the primer, some primers fill up pores to make a smooth application base. Foundation corrects the tone of your face. Concealer conceals your dark under eyes and other blemishes. Powder sets your liquid bases so they won't move. Lastly, blush creates a pinkish effect on the apples of your cheeks so it won't look too flat.

SCULPTING PRODUCTS


Contour and adding some warmth on the face creates dimension, depth and copy the natural shadows from our faces. Adding a highlight would also be a good benefit because it gives a shine on the face so the face won't look totally flat matte.

EYELASHES


Making the eyeslashes look fuller make your makeup more alive. First step is to curl your eyelashes. It makes your lashes lifted and more visible. Mascara is simply coating your eyelashes with black liquid gel to have more volume and length. If mascara is not enough, you can add false eyelashes to make your eyes more dramatic and fuller, and glue it with the eyelash glue
