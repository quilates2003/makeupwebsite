Brushes and Sponges

Right tool makes your makeup seamlessly beautiful.

FLAT TOP BRUSH

Apply a decent amount of foundation and blend it using a brush to have more coverage. This flat top brush is good for beginners in blending the foundation seamlessly. It can be either stipple bounce off and on the skin or buff in circular motion.

CONCEALER BRUSH

For concealing, this concealer brush helps to distribute the concealer under the eyes equally, but it is most used for spot concealing. Conceal only the exact place of the blemish you're only concern to.

CONTOUR BRUSH

Using an angled contouring brush makes the contour more reliable and easy to use for beginners. Its angled shape helps to apply the contour on specific areas creating a seamless line for adding depth in the face.

BLUSH BRUSH

A soft rounded brush helps to distribute the blush easily because it fits exactly on the cheeks.

FAN BRUSH

A fan brush for a more precise highlight on the top of the blush.

POWDER BRUSH

A tapered powder brush helps more to reach the corners of the undereye when dusting off powder.

EYE BLENDING BRUSH

To blend the eyeshadow especially on the outer corner of the eye, use a big fluffy eyeshadow brush. In this way, it disperse the transition or eyeshadow easier.

FLAT SHADER BRUSH

This brush is used to apply eyeshadow color in one place. It is commonly used to pack on shimmer pigments on the eyelids.

SMALL ANGLED BRUSH

This brush is used to fill the spars areas in the eyebrow. the angled shape makes it easier to fill the brows.

SMALL BLENDING BRUSH

This is used to blend in more precise area like betweek two different eyeshadow color. It can also apply darker color on the outer corner of the eyelid.

BLENDING SPONGE

Blending is a very important part of a makeup routine. Blending sponges make it possible for a smoother makeup result. It also tends to absorb more product that result in reducing the coverage of the product applied.

MINI MAKEUP SPONGE

Mini sponges make it the same way as well. It's way more smaller than a usual makeup sponge. It is specifically made to blend concealer because sometimes it's hard to reach and blend the corners under the eye by a usual makeup sponge.
